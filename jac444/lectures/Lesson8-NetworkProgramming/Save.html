Canada Day Long Weekend

Seneca will observe the Canada Day holiday with an extra long weekend, closing on June 30 and July 1. Here's what the rest of the weekend looks like:

Friday, June 27 evening:

  • Continuing Education classes will be held.

Saturday, June 28 and Sunday, June 29:

  • Continuing Education classes will be held.
  • The Seneca@York Campus Library and Computing Commons will be closed.
  • The Newnham Campus Library and Computing Commons will be open.

Monday, June 30 and Tuesday, July 1:

  • The College will be closed, no classes will be held.

Wednesday, July 2:

  • Classes and all regular activities will resume.

All ICT Graduating Students - Opportunity for soon-to-be graduates

Get an edge in the IT industry with our new Graduate Certificate program

As a recent ICT graduate, you know the benefits of studying at Seneca - a leader in IT education. Now you can enhance your credentials with a short graduate certificate program, Project Management – Information Technology (PMC). PMC is specifically designed for students with a degree or diploma in electronics, software development or IT administration.

This full-time 8-month program prepares you with the requisite skills to work on IT projects in a project management role. You will also be provided with the theory to write the qualifying exams for Project Management Institute credentials – giving you an advantage in your career.

Graduate as a career-ready professional with in-demand industry skills.

CLASSES START SEPTEMBER 2014.
