Course Structure

  • 1 Weekly Lecture
  • 1 Weekly Workshop

 

 

 

Course Evaluation

  • Assignments (3) - 30%
  • Tests (2) - 30%
  • Final Exam - 30%

 

Promotion Policy

To obtain a credit in this subject you must achieve the following:

  • 50% on the final exam
  • 50% weighted average on tests and exam
  • 50% on the overall course

Tests and Exam

  • Open Book Policy: You are allowed to use one reference sheet (2-sided, typed and named) and one book on Java programming when you take a test and the final examination.
  • If you miss a test due to an emergency you must email me immediately in order to receive consideration for your absence. You must provide documented evidence (e.g. a doctor's note) of the emergency. No makeup test will be given. There is no make-up test.

Lectures

  • Assignments will not be accepted after the last day of class.
  • Late assignments will be penalized by -10% perday. The maximum late penalty is -100%.
  • Incorrect assignments that need to be resubmitted will receive a maximum grade of 50%.
  • It must be resubmitted within a week upon notification. Afterwards it will receive a ZERO.
